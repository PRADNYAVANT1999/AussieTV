About AussieTV

AussieTV is a random video chat platform that connects people from around the world. Our mission is to create a safe and fun environment for global conversations.

Our Features

  • Random video connections with people worldwide
  • Safe and moderated environment
  • Text chat alongside video
  • Easy-to-use controls
  • Mobile and desktop friendly
  • No registration required

Safety Tips

Stay Safe While Video Chatting

  • Never share personal information like your full name, address, or phone number
  • Report inappropriate behavior immediately
  • Use the skip function if you feel uncomfortable
  • Remember that conversations are not recorded, but screenshots are possible
  • Be respectful to other users
  • If you're under 18, make sure you have parental permission

Community Guidelines

  • No nudity or sexually explicit content
  • No harassment or bullying
  • No spam or commercial content
  • Respect others' privacy
  • Use appropriate language

For camera, microphone, or connection issues:

  • Check your camera and microphone permissions
